Lap Services our services

At Cosmetic Smile Studio, we offer a comprehensive range of high-quality dental prosthetic services designed to meet the unique needs of dental professionals and their patients. Our skilled technicians are committed to delivering precision, aesthetics, and functionality in every product we create. Here’s an overview of the services we provide:

Crowns and Bridges

We specialize in creating custom crowns and bridges that restore function and aesthetics. Using advanced materials such as zirconia, porcelain-fused-to-metal (PFM), and full ceramic options, we ensure strength, durability, and a natural appearance that blends seamlessly with the patient's existing teeth.

Veneers

Our lab crafts beautifully natural-looking veneers that help enhance smiles by correcting imperfections such as discoloration, gaps, or misalignments. Each veneer is custom-made to match the patient's tooth color, shape, and size for a flawless finish.

Implant-Supported Restorations

We offer a range of implant-supported restorations, including single crowns, bridges, and full-arch solutions. Our implant restorations are designed for optimal function, comfort, and esthetics, providing patients with a reliable and long-lasting solution.

Full and Partial Dentures( in digital way)

Whether it's a full or partial denture, our lab creates prosthetics that are both functional and comfortable. We use premium materials and meticulous craftsmanship to ensure a perfect fit and natural appearance, helping patients regain confidence in their smiles.

All-on-4 and All-on-6 Restorations

For patients requiring full-mouth rehabilitation, we offer All-on-4 and All-on-6 restorations that provide a stable and naturallooking solution. These restorations are designed to maximize the use of available bone, ensuring a secure fit and a beautiful smile.

Full and Partial Dentures( in digital way)

Equipped with cutting-edge CAD/CAM technology, we provide digital design and milling services to create precise and efficient dental restorations. This digital workflow ensures consistent quality and faster turnaround times.

Custom Shade Matching

We understand the importance of color matching in dental prosthetics. Our lab offers custom shade matching services to ensure each restoration harmonizes perfectly with the patient's natural teeth, providing an indistinguishable blend.
